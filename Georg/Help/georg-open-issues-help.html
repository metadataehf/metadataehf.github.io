Georg - Open Issues List

This page displays all open complaints and claims in your system that require attention. Open issues include those with status "New," "Processing," and "On Hold."

Important: Regular review of open issues is essential to ensure nothing falls through the cracks. Consider scheduling weekly reviews of all open complaints to maintain timely resolutions.

Working with the Open Issues List

The Open Issues list provides a comprehensive view of all active complaints that need attention. From this view, you can:

View Complaint Details

Click on any issue in the list to open its full details. This allows you to review all information about the complaint and make updates as needed.

Filter and Sort

Use the filter fields at the top of each column to find specific issues based on criteria like customer name, reason, or date. Click on column headers to sort the list in ascending or descending order.

Export to Excel

Use the export option to analyze data in Microsoft Excel. This is useful for creating custom reports or sharing information with team members who don't have access to Business Central.

Understanding Issue Statuses

Status Description Action Required
New Recently registered issue that hasn't been started Change to "Processing" when work begins
Processing Active issue currently being worked on Document actions in the "Resolution Actions" section
On Hold Issue waiting for information or external action Document reason for hold and expected timeframe

Managing Issue Progress

When you open an issue from this list, you can:

  1. Update the incident details with new information as it becomes available
  2. Add resolution actions that have been taken to address the complaint
  3. Change the status as the issue progresses through your resolution process
  4. Add additional information or detailed descriptions

Note: The "Open Issues" list automatically refreshes when you return to it, showing the most current information about all active complaints.

Pro Tip: Monitor response times by comparing registration dates with the current date for open issues. This can help identify bottlenecks in your resolution process and ensure you're meeting your service level agreements (SLAs).
