Georg - Claims & Complaints Overview

Welcome to Georg, your all-in-one solution for efficiently managing customer complaints and claims within Microsoft Business Central. Georg facilitates the registration, tracking, and follow-up of customer issues, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks in your service quality management.

What You Can Do From This Page

The Georg main menu serves as your central hub for all complaint and claim management activities. From here, you can access all the key functions of the system:

Create a New Issue

Start recording a new customer complaint or claim. Georg will automatically assign the next number in your configured number series. Learn more about creating a new complaint.

View Open Issues

Access and manage all currently active complaints that require attention, whether they're new, in processing, or on hold.
Learn more about managing open issues.

View Closed Issues

Review previously resolved complaints for reference, analysis, and reporting purposes. Learn more about closed issues.

Setup Default Values

Configure the system for first-time use, including number series and master data. Learn more about setup configuration.

You can also access master data configuration for Business Units, Reasons, Origins, Categories, and Types directly from the main menu.

Note: If this is your first time using Georg, we recommend starting with "Setup Default Values" to configure your number series and import default master data values.

Getting Started

To begin managing claims and complaints with Georg:

  1. Ensure default values are set up (if not already done) - Configure master data setup
  2. Click "Create a New Issue" to register your first complaint
  3. Fill in the required information such as customer details, reason, and category
  4. Change the status to "Processing" once you begin working on the issue
  5. Document resolution actions as you work through the complaint
  6. Close the issue once it's fully resolved

Pro Tip: Establish team standards for how incidents and resolutions should be documented to ensure consistency across your organization. This will make reporting and analysis much more effective.

Finding Georg in Business Central

You can quickly access Georg at any time by:

  1. Launching your Microsoft Business Central application
  2. Typing either "Georg" or "claims" in the search bar at the top
  3. Clicking on "Georg - Claims & Complaints" when it appears in the dropdown menu
