Georg - Creating a New Complaint

Back to Main Help Page

This page allows you to register new customer complaints or claims in the system. Each complaint is automatically assigned the next number in your configured number series.

Starting a New Complaint

To create a new complaint or claim:

From the Georg main menu, click "Create a New Issue"

The system will automatically open a new complaint card with the next available number from your series

Fill in the required fields (marked with *)

Change the status to "Processing" when you begin actively working on the issue

Required Information

To create a new complaint, you need to fill in these essential details:

* Registration Date
When the complaint was received (defaults to today)
* Registered By
The employee who received the complaint
* Business Unit
The department responsible for handling the issue
* Customer Number/Name
The customer who submitted the complaint
* Origin
How the complaint was received (e.g., CUSTOMER, EMAIL, PHONE)
* Reason
The primary reason for the complaint (e.g., INVOICE, DELIVERY)
* Category
The category for classification (e.g., BILLING, SHIPPING)

Note: Some fields may be automatically filled in with default values. Review these to ensure they're appropriate for the current complaint.

Complaint Form Sections

Incident Logging

Use this section to document what happened and who was involved. This section should include:

  • Detailed description of the incident
  • The root cause (if known)
  • How the complaint was received
  • Who was involved in the incident

Thorough documentation here helps with understanding the issue and preventing similar problems in the future.

Resolution Actions

Document all steps taken to resolve the issue, including:

  • Specific actions performed to address the complaint
  • Who performed each action
  • When each action was completed
  • Results of each action

This section creates an audit trail of all efforts made to resolve the customer's issue.

Additional Information

Use this section for any relevant details that don't fit in the other sections, such as:

  • Related documents or order numbers
  • External communications
  • Special circumstances

Descriptions

Space for more detailed explanations if needed. This can include:

  • Extended narrative of complex situations
  • Customer feedback verbatim
  • Detailed technical explanations

Managing Complaint Status

Each complaint can have one of the following statuses:

New Processing On Hold Closed

After filling in the initial information:

  • Click "Change Status to Processing" to indicate active work has begun
  • Use "Change Status to On Hold" if you're waiting for information or external action
  • Select "Change Status to Closed" once the issue is fully resolved

Pro Tip: Be thorough in your documentation. Complete information makes it easier to track patterns and implement systemic improvements. Consider standardizing how your team documents complaints to ensure consistency.
