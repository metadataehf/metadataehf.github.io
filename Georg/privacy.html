Privacy Statement

Introduction

Metadata ehf ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your data. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our Georg - Claims & Complaints application ("Georg" or the "Application") for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

By using Georg, you agree to the data practices described in this statement.

Information We Collect

Customer Data

Georg collects and processes the following types of information as part of its operation:

  • Customer contact information (name, company, email, phone number)
  • Complaint and claim details, including registration dates, descriptions, and resolutions
  • Business unit and user information for tracking and assignment purposes
  • Categorization data such as complaint origins, reasons, and categories
  • Resolution actions and steps taken to address issues
  • Additional information provided during the complaint handling process

Usage Data

We may collect anonymized usage data about how the Application is being used to improve our service and troubleshoot issues. This may include:

  • Feature usage statistics
  • Performance metrics
  • Error reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Georg Application's functionality
  • To enable you to register, track, and resolve customer complaints and claims
  • To improve our Application based on usage patterns and feedback
  • To provide technical support and troubleshoot issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Processing

All customer data is stored and processed within your Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central environment. We do not extract or store your data on our servers unless specifically authorized for troubleshooting or support purposes. The Georg Application operates entirely within your Business Central instance, utilizing Microsoft's secure infrastructure for data storage.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties. We may share information under the following circumstances:

  • With Microsoft as the platform provider for Business Central
  • With our authorized service providers who help us operate, maintain, and support the Application
  • When required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Metadata ehf, our customers, or others

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These security measures include:

  • Leveraging Microsoft's secure Business Central platform
  • Digital signing of our application files
  • Implementation of permission sets to control access to data
  • Regular security assessments and updates

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Retention

Customer data is retained within your Business Central environment according to your organization's data retention policies. The Georg Application does not implement separate data retention periods outside of your Business Central settings.

International Data Transfers

As the Application operates within your Business Central environment, data transfers follow Microsoft's data transfer and storage practices. For information about Business Central's compliance with international data transfer regulations, please refer to Microsoft's privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the revised statement on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this statement periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
